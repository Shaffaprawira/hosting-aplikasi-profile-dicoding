Welcome to My Website!

I am a computer engineering student from the University of Indonesia and I am thrilled to share with you my journey as a participant in the Cloud Computing Learning Path of Bangkit 2023.

For those who are unfamiliar, Bangkit is a prestigious career readiness program that aims to develop top-notch technical talent for leading Indonesian technology companies and startups. The program is fully supported by three of Indonesia's tech giants - Google, GoTo, and Traveloka - making it an incredible opportunity for me and my fellow participants to learn from the best and gain invaluable industry exposure.

As a participant in the Cloud Computing Learning Path, I am excited to delve deeper into the world of cloud technology and explore its vast potential. I look forward to learning from seasoned experts, collaborating with like-minded individuals, and building practical skills that will enable me to make a meaningful impact in the tech industry.

Through this website, I hope to document my progress, share my insights, and showcase my achievements as I navigate this incredible learning journey. So join me as I embark on this exciting adventure and discover the endless possibilities of cloud computing!
